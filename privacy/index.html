Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

BumbleTasks

👋 Overview

BumbleTasks ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your family's privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties — ever.

📋 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).

Family & Profile Data: Family name, child profile names, ages, emoji avatars, and points balances that you create within the App.

Task & Reward Data: Tasks you create, assign, and complete; rewards you define; and point transaction history.

Usage Data: Crash reports and basic usage analytics (via Sentry) to help us improve the App. This data is anonymized and does not include personal identifiers.

👧 Children's Privacy

BumbleTasks is intended for use by parents and guardians to manage their children's tasks. Child profiles within the App are created and controlled entirely by the parent/guardian.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.
  • Child profiles contain only the information you (the parent) provide: a name, age, and emoji avatar.
  • Children do not create accounts or register with BumbleTasks independently.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us without parental consent, please contact us at support@bumbletasks.com and we will promptly delete it.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features (tasks, rewards, profiles).
  • To send real-time updates within your family (via Supabase Realtime).
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs (via Sentry).
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not use your data for advertising.

🤝 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate BumbleTasks:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and real-time sync. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Error monitoring and crash reporting. Privacy Policy

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Access to your family's data is restricted by Row-Level Security policies in our database.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use industry-standard protections.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your family's data at any time:

  • Use the "Delete Family & Data" option in Settings.
  • Or email us at support@bumbletasks.com and we'll delete your data within 30 days.

📬 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice or email. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
